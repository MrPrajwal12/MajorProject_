    Personolized fitness application for staying healthy

    Our program combines cutting-edge computer vision techniques to concentrate on Human Pose Estimation. It entails precisely identifying from video data the locations and orientations of a person's bodily joints. We do this by using a cutting-edge deep learning model called MoveNet that was created exclusively for real-time pose estimation. By utilizing its complex design and MobileNetV2 basis, MoveNet demonstrates excellent efficiency. MoveNet enables the accurate and dependable estimate of human poses by deftly analyzing the spatial interactions between distinct body parts. Our application becomes much more versatile and effective because to this capabilities, which opens up a wide range of interactive and immersive experiences.

    Progress tracking and analytics to monitor your results
